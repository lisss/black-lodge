# Quest Answers (For Testing)

## Stage 1: Welcome to the Investigation
**Question**: What two words did Morrison write?
**Backward text**: ".egdoL kcalB"
**Answer**: `black lodge` or `blacklodge`
**Hint**: Read it backwards (right to left)

## Stage 2: The First Photograph
**Type**: Choice
**Options**:
- This deserves investigation. Morrison was onto something.
- It's probably just a photo development error.

## Stage 3: Morrison's Favorite Puzzle
**Question**: What does the message say? (two words, no spaces)
**Cipher**: 8-5-12-12-15 / 6-18-9-5-14-4
**Solution**: A=1, B=2, C=3... so 8=H, 5=E, 12=L, 12=L, 15=O, 6=F, 18=R, 9=I, 5=E, 14=N, 4=D
**Answer**: `hello friend` or `hellofriend`

## Stage 4: The Pattern Emerges
**Type**: Choice
**Options**:
- Continue the investigation. This is getting interesting.
- Something supernatural is happening here.

## Stage 5: Morrison's Last Message
**Question**: What were Morrison's final words? (four words, no spaces)
**Backward text**: "...kcab emoc t'now I"
**Answer**: `i wont come back` or `iwontcomeback`
**Hint**: Read it backwards

## Stage 6: Your Decision
**Type**: Final Choice
**Options**:
- Seal the files. Morrison is lost. We must move on.
- The investigation should continue. Understanding this is important.
- Morrison left us clues for a reason. Honor that courage.

All choices lead to quest completion!
